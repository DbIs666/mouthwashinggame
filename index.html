Mouthwashing Game Overview

Setting & Background

Mouthwashing Game takes place on the Tulpar, a long-haul space freighter that has become marooned after a catastrophic crash. The crew faces dwindling supplies and psychological tension as they grapple with their dire situation. As resources become scarce and isolation takes its toll, the line between reality and nightmare begins to blur.

Players experience a nonlinear story that unfolds through the perspectives of different crew members, primarily focusing on Captain Curly, co-pilot Jimmy, and the mysterious Mouthwashing incident. The narrative weaves together multiple timelines and viewpoints, creating a complex tapestry of events that led to the current crisis.
